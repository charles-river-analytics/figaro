com.cra.figaro.example.dosage

DosageDecision

object DosageDecision

The effectiveness of the drug is a function of the dosage. The base effectiveness of the drug is if the protein has mutated less than the 10% of the self-protein distance (in the mutation matrices, the distance between the same Amino Acids is not zero) Each dosage of the drug raises the effectiveness threshold
